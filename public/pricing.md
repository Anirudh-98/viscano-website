## How Viscano Prices Projects

All Viscano projects are scoped from a brief or PRD — no fixed tiers. You only pay for what you actually need.

## Why No Standard Packages?

No two projects are the same. A ₹50K logo and a ₹5L brand platform both start with "logo" but require completely different processes. Scoping from a brief ensures accuracy and prevents scope creep.

1. **Brief or PRD** — Share what you're building (Notion, PDF, or voice note)
2. **Discovery call** — 30-minute focused conversation
3. **Scoped proposal** — Deliverables, timeline, and fixed quote
4. **Kickoff & delivery** — Weekly check-ins and milestone reviews

## FAQs

**What is a PRD and do I need one?**  
A Product Requirements Document outlines what you're building, for whom, and why. You don't need a formal PRD — a Notion doc, a brief, or a detailed conversation works.

**Do you offer payment in installments?**  
Yes. Standard split is 50% on kickoff and 50% on final delivery. Larger projects are milestone-based.

**Do you work with international clients?**  
Yes. We work with clients across India and internationally. We accept wire transfers and USD invoicing for international projects.

**What if I need something not listed?**  
Get in touch. If we can't do it, we'll tell you. If we can, we'll scope it.
