## Service Overview

User interfaces and experiences designed around real user behaviour — not assumptions. From research and wireframes through to high-fidelity prototypes and production-ready Figma handoff.

## What We Do

We design digital products that people actually understand and enjoy using. Our process starts with research — understanding who your users are, what they're trying to do, and where existing solutions fail them. From there, we move through information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and final UI design.

Every design we deliver is production-ready: Figma files with components, variables, and developer annotations so your engineering team can build without ambiguity.

## Deliverables

- User Research & Interviews
- User Flows & Information Architecture
- Wireframes (Low & Mid Fidelity)
- Interactive Prototypes
- High-Fidelity UI Screens
- Design System (Components, Typography, Color)
- Figma Handoff with Developer Annotations
- Usability Testing & Iteration
