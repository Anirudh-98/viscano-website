1. **Growth Audit** — Forensic review of your current marketing: ad accounts, SEO baseline, email sequences, and conversion funnel.
2. **Strategy & Forecast** — A 90-day marketing roadmap with channel mix, budget allocation, and projected outcomes.
3. **Creative & Setup** — Ad creative, landing pages, email sequences, and campaign structure built and reviewed before launch.
4. **Launch & Optimise** — Live within two weeks, then weekly optimisation: bid adjustments, creative rotation, audience refinement.
5. **Scale & Report** — Monthly strategy reviews to double down on winning channels and cut underperformers.
