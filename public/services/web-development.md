## Our Process

1. **Discovery & Scoping** — We map out your goals, audience, content structure, and technical requirements in a detailed scope document.
2. **Design & Prototype** — Our designers build wireframes and high-fidelity mockups that you review and approve before a single line of code is written.
3. **Development Sprints** — We build in two-week sprints with weekly demos on a live staging environment so you can give real feedback in real time.
4. **QA & Optimization** — Cross-browser testing, accessibility audit, performance optimization, and final content review before launch.
5. **Launch & Handoff** — Zero-downtime deployment, DNS cutover support, and a full walkthrough of the CMS so your team can own the content.
